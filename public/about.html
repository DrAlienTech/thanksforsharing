    • About Us

    We have everything. Yes, that's right. This rental company isn't limited to a specific type of product. We have a variety of selections for users to choose. Our mission statement is to create a great rental experience for everyone.

    Why Choose Us

    The fear that many people have with renting is that the products will be unsatisfactory or be old and overused by previous renters. While we can not guarantee that your order is perfect, we can guarantee a full refund if you email us about your order with details and proof.
    Our C2C program is top-notch, with a simple interface that will allow you to share your items with others without stressing over the safety of the item. To ensure this, we have customers pay for the first period upfront, so you are guaranteed that the customer can pay. As with our customer relations, you can email us to discuss any problems or fears you may have.
    The best part? We're all local! We support the Forsyth County community

    Contact Info

    You can contact us through our email. You can also contact us through Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram and follow us for the latest updates.
